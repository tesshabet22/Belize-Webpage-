About Belize

  • Belize is part of two regions: Central America and the Caribbean
  • It has a population of about 400,000 people
  • There are many cultures such as Mestizo, Mayan, Creole, Garifuna, Hispanic, Lebanese, Mennonite, Chinese, East Indian and more
  • The Capital of Belize is Belmopan
  • The Official language of Belize is English with most people being trilingual speaking English, Spanish and English Creole(a dialect of English)
  • Belize is geographically diverse with many jungles, beaches, mountains, rivers, reefs, cayes and more
  • The avergage annual temperature is 80 degrees Fahrenheit

Why You Should Visit

Racial harmony, religious tolerance and a relatively peaceful political culture has given Belize a reputation as a friendly, laidback destination. Belize is one of Central America's most popular tourist destinations. It is delightfully accessible to American travellers because the official language is English and the dollar is the main currency making monetary conversions easy. There is a lot to see and a lot to do. From snorkelling in the cayes to hiking in the mountains with the opportunities to spot jaguars and other unique wildlife, you will have experiences unlike any other.
